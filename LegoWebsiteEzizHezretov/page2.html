Fun Facts About Legos

  • The name "LEGO" is an abbreviation of the Danish words "leg godt," which means "play well".
  • Somehow there are more than 600 billion Lego bricks in the world.
  • LEGO is the world's biggest tire manufacturer, it makes over 300 million rubber tires each year.
